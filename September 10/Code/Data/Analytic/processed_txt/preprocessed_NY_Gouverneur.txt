restricting the height number of stories and size of buildings and other structures the percentage of lots that may be occupied the size of yards courts and other open spaces the density of population of the districts and their peculiar suitability for particular uses and with a view to conserving the value of buildings and encouraging the most appropriate use of land throughout the village
